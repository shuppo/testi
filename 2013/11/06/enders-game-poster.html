06 Nov 2013

Enders Game

Screen Print Poster

About four years ago I finished one of my favorite books, again. Being the young scrappy designer I was at the time, I designed this poster for the love of the story and the character Ender. Now, fast forward to two weeks ago, when I got a surprise email from Ryan, who had been checking in from time to time to see if I'd ever gotten around to printing it. I asked if he'd like to print it, and he agreed. We produced a small run of 55 individually numbered posters. I hope you snatch one up!
